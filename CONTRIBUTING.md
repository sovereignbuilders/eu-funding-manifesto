# Contributing to Sovereign Builders

This is a living policy document. It grows stronger with every founder who adds their voice, their data, and their experience to it.

There are three ways to contribute — choose the one that fits your comfort level.

---

## Level 1 — Sign the Manifesto

Open `SIGNATORIES.md` and add one line in this format:

| Your Name | Country | What you build | Link (optional) |

Then submit a Pull Request with the title: `Signatory: [Your Name] — [Country]`

Not familiar with Pull Requests? File an Issue titled `Sign: [Your Name] — [Country]` and we will add you manually.

---

## Level 2 — Add a Quote

If you are willing to have your words attached to your name in media outreach and policy submissions, open an Issue titled:

`Quote: [Your Name] — [Country]`

Include one or two sentences describing your personal experience with EU funding exclusion. Be specific. Specific is powerful.

Example: "As a solo developer in Portugal, I spent three weeks understanding why I cannot access a programme I theoretically qualify for — then another week finding out I need a partner organisation in a second country before I can even apply."

---

## Level 3 — Strengthen the Evidence

If you have country-specific data, a documented policy barrier, or a use case not yet in the framework — open a Pull Request targeting `EVIDENCE.md`.

Format:

| Claim | Source | Country | Year |

Every verified data point added here strengthens the case when this document reaches ITRE Committee desks.

---

## What We Do Not Accept

- Self-promotion or product pitches
- Content unrelated to EU innovation funding policy
- Unverified or unciteable claims in EVIDENCE.md
