# Sovereign Builders
## A Policy Framework for EU Innovation Funding Reform

> *Built by and for independent European software founders.*

---

The European Union funds innovation.  
What it does not fund — by design, not by accident — is the builder who needs no institution around them to build.

In the first half of 2025, **over one third of all new companies globally were founded by a single person.**  
Solo founders represent **30% of all startups founded in 2024** and received **14.7% of available capital.**  
The EU funding architecture was not built for them. It has not been updated for them.

This repository is the collective demand to change that.

---

## The Document

**[Architecting a Leverage-Native Ecosystem](./whitepaper.md)**  
A structured policy framework identifying four structural impasses in EU innovation funding and the precise amendments required to fix them — timed to the active FP10 negotiation window (2026).

---

## The Four Demands

**1. Decouple eligibility from headcount.**  
Measure output per capita, not employees.

**2. Eliminate the consortium requirement** for early-stage solo operators.  
Fund the builder, not the bureaucratic structure around them.

**3. Replace process-based compliance** with milestone-driven, output-verified disbursement.  
Reclaim the 30% of founder time currently lost to timesheets.

**4. Deploy code-first micro-grants** of €50k–€100k evaluated on demonstrated execution, not speculative prose.  
Let the repository speak.
